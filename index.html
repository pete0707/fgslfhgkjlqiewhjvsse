Chatbot Experience Research

Please read the following instructions and start talking with the chatbot:

- To start, please click the bottom-right message icon -> "Start chat" -> "Continue as guest".

- To interact with the chatbot, please simply click on the buttons showing up on the chatbot interface.

- Please note that the chatbot does not expect any response except button clicking when telling the story. Please simply follow the story and wait for the buttons to show up.

- If you have questions about the story, please simply click the button "I have a question". Otherwise, the chatbot expects you to click the "continue" button, which will then trigger a question for attention check and the story will continue afterwards.

- Please send your messages only when you clicked the button "I have a question" and prompted by the chatbot to share your thoughts.

These will help ensure a smoother conversation and improve the quality of the interaction.

Thank you!
