Chatbot Experience Research

Please read the following instructions and start talking with the chatbot:

- To start, please click the bottom-right message icon -> "Start chat" -> "Continue as guest".

- To interact with the chatbot, you can simply click on the buttons showing up on the chatbot interface, or type your messages in the chat box.

- While buttons may be displayed on the chatbot interface, it is still allowed to enter your own messages. However, if the chatbot expects you to respond by clicking a button, it will let you know after you have provided your input.

- Please note that your message to the chatbot should be given in a single, complete message when interacting with the chatbot. Avoid dividing it into multiple parts.

- Please send your messages only when prompted by the chatbot with a question or a request to share your thoughts.

These will help ensure a smoother conversation and improve the quality of the interaction.

Thank you!
